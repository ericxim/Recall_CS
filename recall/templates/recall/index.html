What is Recall Media Studies?

Recall Media is a website aimed at helping indiviuals with their understanding of the subject Media Studies by giving them a more streamlined method of accessing the relevant and useful information/resources. Users have the capability to join communities and contribute towards them, in addition community admins have the ability to assign questions and assess the performance of each member.

What is Media Studies?

Media Studies is the theoretical analysis of Media, there are many theorist that have studied the properties of Media such as how it is displayed and spread throughout society. This gives individuals to ability to be more aware and skeptical of what is being shared around.
